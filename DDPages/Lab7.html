Brief Intro

The purpose of this website is to give you basic facts about Spider-Man, such as costumes and villains he has. Spider-Man is a very popular character in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. There are movies, games, books, tv shows, and more dedicated to the beloved character.

Ten Quick Spider-Man facts

  1. Was created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko
  2. His name is Peter Parker
  3. He was bitten by a radioactive spider
  4. He has powers such as web slinging and spider sense
  5. He was raised by his uncle and aunt
  6. He has an aunt named Aunt May
  7. His biological parents are called Richard and Mary Parker
  8. He has a best friend called Harry Osborn
  9. He works at the Daily Bugle as a photographer
  10. From New York City
